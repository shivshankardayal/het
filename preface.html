Preface

All that glitters is not gold.

You do not judge a book by its cover. If you are a regular visitor then you would know that I ran libreprogramming.org and 10hash.com. You would also know that I never liked SOPA or PIPA and the way in the name of legal and copyright issues books and knowledge is being hoarded.

I am a voracious reader and writer. I read a lot and write a lot. After reading a lot of books and trying to teach quite a few students I started writing a C Book which is almost complete but will never complete. To write a book I learned several typesetting systems like Groff, LaTeX, Docbook, Sphinx etc but was never satisfied by results. I had also decided that I will write books on math while I struggled to explain complexity algorithms as I could not make assumptions. Now this led to huge burden of writing several books. Therefore, I decided to write one book which will answer all questions. Please have patience and in due course of time it will be all clear.

Why I wrote this book?

So that no one should experience this pain which I have experienced while teaching young students. This book is more like notes to me.

Who should read this book?

Anyone can read first chapter about basics but things get quite complicated after that fast. Although, it is written for someone like \(10\) years old but you will find that the material is quite complex in nature. It is not for the faint-hearted one. So steel your heart before proceeding.

Prerequisite

Class \(5\) education is expected. The more you know the more you need to read. Access to a computer running GNU/Linux with internet access is needed. Basic familiarity with executing commands is also assumed.
